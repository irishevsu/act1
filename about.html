About Me

Hi, I'm Irish!

Born and raised in the Philippines, I started pursuing my passion for design and marketing at a young age. Now at 21, I already have strong 5 years of graphic design and digital marketing experience.

Working at medium-sized digital marketing agencies as a freelance graphic designer for years improved not only my design skills but also my people skills.

Throughout the years, I formed relationships with businesses all over the world, helping them stand out and grow their influence through the power of design and social media marketing. I specialize in content creation & strategy, organic social media growth, and engagement. I always do my best to provide an exceptional experience to my clients because when I commit, I give my 100%.
